Describes a tap changer with a linear relation between the tap step and the phase angle difference across the transformer. This is a mathematical model that is an approximation of a real phase tap changer.<br /></p><p>The phase angle is computed as stepPhaseShitfIncrement times the tap position.<br /></p><p>The secondary side voltage magnitude is the same as at the primary side.<br /></p>
