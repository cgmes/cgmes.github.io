The class represents IEEE Std 421.5-2005 type PSS1A power system stabilizer model. PSS1A is the generalized form of a PSS with a single input. Some common stabilizer input signals are speed, frequency, and power.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Reference: IEEE 1A 421.5-2005 Section 8.1. <br /></p>
