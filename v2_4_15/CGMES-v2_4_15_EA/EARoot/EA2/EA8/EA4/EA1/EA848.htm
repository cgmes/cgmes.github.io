The Subtransient Round Rotor Type is modelled using one of the concrete child classes of the SynchronousMachineDetailed class.    If the SynchronousMachineTimeConstantReactance class is used, .modelType = subtransient and .rotorType = roundRotor.<br /></p><p>The complete equivalent circuit is used with two rotor windings in each axis. <br /></p><p><font color="#0f0f0f">Ifd represents LadIfd (XadIfd) and is a voltage proportional to field current in the per unit system ( IEEE 421.5-2005, Annex B).  It is equal to Efd in steady-state.  </font><br /></p><p><b>Parameter Notes:</b><br /></p><p><ol><li><font color="#0f0f0f"><b>X''q</b> may be assumed to be equal to <b>X''d</b> (no subtransient saliency).</font></li><br /></p><p><li><font color="#0f0f0f">Saturation is modeled in both the d and q axes.</font></li><br /></p><p><li><font color="#0f0f0f">For generators, the following parameters may be omitted with defaults shown: <b>X''q</b> (=<b>X''d</b>), <b>Ks</b> (=0), <b>S1q</b> (=<b>S1</b>), <b>S12q</b> (=<b>S12</b>), <b>Tc</b> (=0).</font></li><br /></p><p><li><font color="#0f0f0f">For motors, the following parameter is not used: <b>X''q</b>. </font></li><br /></p><p></ol><br /></p>
