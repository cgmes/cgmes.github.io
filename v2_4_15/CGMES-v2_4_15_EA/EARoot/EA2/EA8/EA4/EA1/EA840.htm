The mechanical equations for all variations of the synchronous motor model are the same and can be represented by the SynchronousMotorMechanicalEquation diagram.<br /></p><p>All variables are per unit on motor MVA base except angle, which is in radians.  <b>omega</b><b><sub>0</sub></b> is the system synchronous frequency in radians per second. <b>omega</b><b><sub>ref</sub></b> is the per unit system frequency (or isolated area frequency), which may be constant at the initial frequency or vary during a simulation depending on the application program.<br /></p>
