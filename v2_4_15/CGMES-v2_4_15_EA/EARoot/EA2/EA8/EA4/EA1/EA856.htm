The Subtransient Round Rotor Simplified Type is modelled using one of the concrete child classes of the SynchronousMachineDetailed class.  If the SynchronousMachineTimeConstantReactance class is used, .modelType = subtransientSimplified and .rotorKind = roundRotor.  <br /></p><p>It is a simplified version of Subtransient Round Rotor Type, magnetic coupling between the direct and quadrature axes is ignored.<br /></p><p><b>Parameter Notes:</b><br /></p><p><ol><li>The effects of speed variation are neglected. </li><br /></p><p><li>No armature resistance.</li><br /></p><p><li>No saturation effects.</li><br /></p><p><li>No subtransient saliency.</li><br /></p><p><li>Direct Axis with field circuit and one equivalent damper circuit, <b>X'd</b> not = <b>X''d</b>.</li><br /></p><p><li>Quadrature Axis with two equivalent damper circuit, <b>X''q</b> not = <b>X'q</b>.</li><br /></p><p><li>For generators and motors the following input parameters are needed: <b>Xd</b>, <b>X'd</b>, <b>X''d</b>, <b>T'do</b>, <b>T''do</b> and <b>Xq</b>, <b>X'q</b>, <b>X''q</b>, <b>T'qo</b>, <b>T''qo</b>.</li><br /></p><p></ol><br /></p>
