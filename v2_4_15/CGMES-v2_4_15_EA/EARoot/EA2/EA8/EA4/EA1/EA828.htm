<b>Parameter Notes:</b><br /></p><p><ol><li>The model is often used for gross equivalents of parts of a system that are not represented in detail.  In this case the MVA rating would be the combined rating of all generators in the equivalenced area.  </li><br /></p><p><li><b>Rs</b> + <b>jXp</b> would be the short circuit equivalent impedance at the location of the equivalent generator on the generator MVA rating base. Inertia constant (<b>H)</b> would be typical or average values for the generators in the equivalenced area.</li><br /></p><p><li>The internal reactance (<b>Xp</b>) may be labelled in different ways (X’, X”, X’d, X”d) by different simulation tools.</li><br /></p><p></ol><br /></p>
