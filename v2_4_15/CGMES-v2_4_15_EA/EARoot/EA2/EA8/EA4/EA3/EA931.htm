<b>Parameter Notes:</b><br /></p><p><ol><li><b>Tt</b>, <b>Tg</b>, <b>Tw</b>, and <b>At</b> must be greater than zero.</li><br /></p><p><li>The nonlinear gain between gate position and power may be input with up to 6 points. The (0.,0.) and (1.,1.) points are assumed and need not be exchanged. The output is not allowed to go beyond 0. and 1.  However, if <b>Pmax</b> #gt; 1., the input and output are scaled by <b>Pmax</b>.  If <b>Gv1</b> is a negative number, the default hydro curve  will be used. If all zero values are provided, a straight line is used.</li><br /></p><p></ol><br /></p>
