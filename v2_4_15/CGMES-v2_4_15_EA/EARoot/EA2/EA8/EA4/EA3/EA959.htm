<font color="#0f0f0f"><b>Parameter Notes:</b></font><br /></p><p><ol><li><b>T3</b> must be greater than zero. <b>T5</b> must be greater than zero if fast valving is used. All other time constants may be zero.</li><br /></p><p><li>The gains <b>K1, K2, K3</b> describe the division of power output among turbine stages. Normally, <b>K1</b>+<b> K2</b> + <b>K3</b> = 1.</li><br /></p><p><li>The times <b>Ta</b>, <b>Tb</b>, and <b>Tc</b> are all with respect to the initiation time.</li><br /></p><p><li>The value <b>Prmax</b> is a limit on the pressure in the reheater. Normally, this value should be about 1.10 p.u.</li><br /></p><p><li>A piece-wise linear characteristic for intercept valve flow vs. position is built in. </li><br /></p><p></ol><br /></p>
