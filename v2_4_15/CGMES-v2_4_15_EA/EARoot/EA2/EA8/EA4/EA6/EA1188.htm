The class represents IEEE Std 421.5-2005 type DC4B model. These excitation systems utilize a field-controlled dc commutator exciter with a continuously acting voltage regulator having supplies obtained from the generator or auxiliary bus.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Reference: IEEE Standard 421.5-2005 Section 5.4. <br /></p>
